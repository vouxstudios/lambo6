I'm Workin'

Born January 7, 1981, in Orlando, Florida, Q6 (also known as Quis and Lambo 6) hails from the Westside of downtown Orlando, specifically “Crosstown.” Q6’s journey into music began in high school at Edgewater Senior High, where he first discovered his passion for rap. His craft flourished during his college years at Auburn University, where he balanced studying Sociology while playing basketball.

Beyond the court, Q6 is a versatile artist whose music transcends the typical athlete-turned-rapper stereotype. His lyrics reflect real-life experiences—stories of struggle, growth, and triumph—that listeners can relate to. Q6 isn’t just about putting words together; he’s about telling his story in a way that resonates with people from all walks of life.
